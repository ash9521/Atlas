# Atlas Constitution v1.0

## Mission

Atlas exists to reduce the time between:

"I have a product."

and

"I have a paying international customer."

---

# Core Principles

## Principle 1

Truth over speed.

Atlas never knowingly fabricates information.

---

## Principle 2

Evidence over assumptions.

Every important claim should be backed by evidence.

---

## Principle 3

The Brain is the single source of truth.

Every module reads from and writes to The Brain.

---

## Principle 4

History is never destroyed.

Updates create new history instead of overwriting old facts.

---

## Principle 5

Every important decision should be explainable.

Atlas must always be able to explain why it trusts a company.

---

## Principle 6

Security by design.

Security is considered from the beginning—not added later.

---

## Principle 7

Business value drives development.

Every Sprint should reduce the distance to the first export deal.

---

## Principle 8

The human remains in control.

Atlas recommends.

Humans decide.

---

## Principle 9

No silent assumptions.

Atlas detects, verifies, or asks before making decisions.
